Money Back Guarantee

Last updated: Sept 25, 2025

If no results* are seen after using Infinium Hair & Skin Care Clinic for the defined eligibility period then you can apply for a refund as per steps below. Please note the number of months for refund is based on your health profile and is not 5 months for all users. Click here to know your eligibility period.

*Results can be defined as hair regrowth, increase in hair volume, reduction of hair fall or reduction of dandruff depending on your initial hair fall stage.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Purchased complete recommended kits from Infinium Hair & Skin Care Clinic website or app.
  2. Purchased the right number of kits in the right time period. For example, if your eligibility period is 5 months, usage of 5 complete kits should happen in a duration of 6 months. This tells us that you have been a regular customer.
  3. Submitted at least 3-4 clear, unshaved scalp images spread across the duration of the Infinium regimen.
  4. Completed at least 3 calls with the Infinium Hair Coach spread across the regimen.
  5. Raised the money-back request within 30 days of purchasing the last kit (as per Infinium treatment plan).
  6. For female customers, the money-back policy applies only for those diagnosed with “medium widening” or “advanced widening” hair loss stages, and who have uploaded a minimum of 3 scalp images over the course of their Infinium treatment.
Kindly note

Certain health conditions, whether pre-existing or developed during the Infinium regimen, make you ineligible for the money back.

Please check the list of conditions before applying.

How to apply?

  • Email us on customercare@infinium.health along with your latest order number.
  • OR raise a ticket for the Money Back Guarantee in the “Help & Support” section, from the “Profile” tab in your Infinium app.

How does the refund process work?

  1. Once your request is raised, we will check for all eligibility conditions listed above.
  2. If eligibility conditions are met, we assign an unbiased and qualified dermatologist to assess your case.
  3. The decision is taken only after you have a call with the assigned dermatologist.

The decision taken by the dermatologist will be final.

Please note that the policy applies only to results seen from the start of the Infinium regimen up to the eligibility period. It does not cover future results or deterioration beyond that period. For example, if your eligibility period is 5 months, the money-back guarantee is only valid for results seen within those 5 months of treatment.

Kindly note that certain health conditions, whether pre-existing or suffered during the Infinium regimen, make you ineligible for the money back.
